Community Safety Tips

Stay informed and protect yourself with these safety tips

1. Stay aware of your surroundings

Be alert and pay attention to what’s happening around you, your surroundings, and your environment, especially in unfamiliar crowds.

2. Report suspicious activities

If you notice anything unusual or suspicious, use Alertnet to report it to the appropriate authorities.

3. Travel safely

Ensure to avoid isolated routes and always tell someone of your destination before embarking on a journey. Travel in groups when possible.

4. Protect your personal issues

Be cautious about sharing sensitive information, especially on public platforms or with strangers.

5. Keep Emergency Numbers Handy

Have quick access to emergency numbers such as the police, fire service, and local security agencies.

6. Secure your property

Ensure that your windows and doors are properly shut when you are leaving the house. If possible, install security cameras.

7. Trust your instincts

If something or someone feels off, ensure to trust your gut and take immediate action to ensure your safety.
