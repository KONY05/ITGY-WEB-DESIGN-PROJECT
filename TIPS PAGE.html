5. Keep Emergency Numbers Handy

6. Secure your property

Ensure that your windows and doors are properly shut when you are leaving the house. If possible, install security cameras.

7. Trust your instincts

If something or someone feels off, ensure to trust your gut and take immediate action to ensure your safety.
