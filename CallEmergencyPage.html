emergency hotline numbers in Nigeria (click number to call)

1. 112 - Nigeria toll-free number

112 serves as Nigeria's official emergency helpline. Managed by NEMA (National Emergency Management Agency), this number provides assistance in both natural and man-made emergencies. When you dial 112, you’ll be connected to relevant emergency response units, including the police, fire service, road safety (FRSC), ambulance services, and more. It’s your direct line for immediate help in any critical situation.

3. 767 - Rapid Response Squad (RRS) for Lagos:

Dial 767 or 112 to report crimes such as theft, robbery, or kidnapping in Lagos. By dialing their dedicated numbers, citizens can report ongoing incidents, suspicious activities, or imminent threats and receive immediate assistance.
