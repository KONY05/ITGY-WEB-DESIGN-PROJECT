REPORT ONLINE

It only takes a few minutes to make a report online. Every year thousands of reports from the public help the police keep communities and families safe from terrorism, kidnapping, and other social vices. Every time you need police assistance in an emergency, contact us immediately.

What should I report?

In Alertnet, reporting suspicious activity is key to ensuring community safety. Users are encouraged to report incidents like criminal activity, violence, public safety concerns, terrorism-related behaviors, potential kidnappings, and threats to infrastructure. Additionally, any unusual or out-of-place behavior should be noted. By providing authorities with detailed information, such as photos and location data, users can help create a safer environment and potentially prevent harmful incidents. Remember, if you see something concerning, report it through Alertnet—your vigilance could make a crucial difference in keeping the community secure.
